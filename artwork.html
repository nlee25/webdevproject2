Artwork for my worldbuilding personal project

I've been drawing since eighth grade and my dream is to make a manga and get it serialized then animated. It's quite a lot of work.

Here are come character designs I partiuclarly liked.

My favorite thing is character design and the thing that drives good design the most, or at least unique design, is the meshing of several distinct themes.

Wren is a mix of mechanoid, catgirl, and maid. Shes a remanant of a dead civilization and houses the soul of the maker of the facility she now protects.

Castiel is the strongest in the militant force of my project. He controls the properties of a single 3 meter slab of stone, but he controls every property completey.

That means the state of matter, current position relative to himself and how many peices it is in. This technicality allows him to be monstrously powerful.

Wormwood is a representative creature of the main antagonistic force in my project. The 'lucid days' are manifestations of 'non-logic' that prey upon the law of life and order.

She is a servant of the 'Trigarchy of the Null', under the master named 'Ubek, the master of the body'.
